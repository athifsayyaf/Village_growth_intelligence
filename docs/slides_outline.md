# Presentation Outline: 5-7 Slides

## Slide 1 - Objective

Identify the top 100 Indian villages with the strongest five-year economic growth signals using satellite and public datasets.

## Slide 2 - Data Sources

Show a table of selected sources: VIIRS night lights, village master, built-up/land cover, roads, and POIs. Explain why each source is relevant.

## Slide 3 - Pipeline

Extraction -> cleaning -> village-level aggregation -> feature engineering -> scoring -> ranking -> visualization.

## Slide 4 - Scoring Method

Define economic growth as combined improvement in light intensity, built-up expansion, road/access improvement, per-capita activity, and amenities. Show the weights.

## Slide 5 - Results

Map and ranked table of top villages. Mention states/districts with clustering.

## Slide 6 - Interpretation

Discuss what the top-ranked villages have in common: rising lights, built-up expansion, better access, and increasing amenities.

## Slide 7 - Limitations and Next Steps

Mention data quality, boundary matching, satellite limitations, and validation with official economic indicators.
